Counseling and Psychological Services

Mental Health and Wellness Support

At the University of Michigan, we take your mental health seriously. Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) offers a range of support options to help you manage stress, deal with personal issues, and enhance your overall well-being.

Individual Counseling

Meet one-on-one with a trained counselor to discuss your concerns in a confidential setting.

Group Therapy and Workshops

Join peer support groups or attend workshops focusing on specific topics like stress management and mindfulness.
